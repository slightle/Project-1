Dramatic shadows are cast as a result of the slot-style canyon, excellent for photographing. Being a photographer myself, I barely did any editing!
Towards the border of New Mexico and Arizona rests Antelope Canyon. It is composed of sandstone that has been eroded by water and wind to develop a magnificent slot canyon.
Ironically, the best viewing of the canyon is in the heat of the day, at high noon, where the sun filters in the top to create profound streams of light.
